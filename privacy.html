Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

Overview

InnerWork is designed with your privacy in mind. Your mental health data is personal and sensitive, and we treat it with the highest level of care. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

The short version: Your data stays on your device. We don't have servers, we don't collect analytics, and we don't share or sell anything. Period.

Data We Collect

InnerWork collects only the data you actively enter into the app, including mood journal entries, thought records, sleep diary entries, activity schedules, and other worksheet responses. The app also records usage metrics such as streak counts and last-used dates to support your progress tracking.

Encryption & Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your encrypted data is stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not operate any external servers and have no access to your data.

Encryption uses AES-256-GCM when the passcode feature is enabled, with the key stored securely in the iOS Keychain.

HealthKit Integration

If you grant permission, the app may read health data (such as sleep and activity metrics) from Apple HealthKit to provide personalized insights. This data is never sent off your device. The app may also write mindfulness session data back to HealthKit. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings.

Location Data

If you grant permission, the app may access your approximate location to fetch local weather and environmental data for mood analytics. Location data is used transiently and is never stored or transmitted to any third party.

Biometric Data

If you enable Face ID or Touch ID, biometric authentication is handled entirely by iOS. The app never accesses, stores, or transmits biometric data. Authentication results are a simple success or failure response.

Speech Recognition

If you use voice journaling, Apple's on-device speech recognition transcribes your spoken words into text. Audio recordings are not stored by the app and transcription happens locally on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third parties. The only data sharing occurs when you explicitly choose to export a report or share a weekly summary, and you control the sharing destination through the standard iOS share sheet.

Analytics & Advertising

InnerWork does not include any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage analytics beyond what Apple provides through App Store Connect, which is anonymous and aggregated.

HIPAA & GDPR

For U.S. users, we follow HIPAA guidelines for protecting health information. For users in the EU, we comply with the GDPR. You have the right to access, correct, and erase your personal data. Use the Data Privacy section in Settings to export or delete your data.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual entries from within the app at any time. To delete all app data, remove the app from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can also delete app data from iCloud via iOS Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.

Children's Privacy

InnerWork is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at hiinnerwork@gmail.com.
