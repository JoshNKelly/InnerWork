Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All data stays on your device (or your personal iCloud if sync is enabled). We don't have servers, we don't collect analytics, and we never share your data. Read our full Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my data?

Open the app, go to Settings, then Data Privacy. You can delete data by category or remove all data at once. Deleting the app also removes all local data.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings, then Data Export. Select the categories you'd like to export, and the app will create a JSON file you can save or share via the standard iOS share sheet.

Does the app replace therapy?

No. InnerWork is a self-help tool based on evidence-based cognitive behavioral therapy techniques. It is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you're struggling, please reach out to a licensed therapist or counselor.

How do I sync across devices?

Go to Settings and enable iCloud sync. Your encrypted data will sync across all your devices signed into the same Apple ID. No account creation needed.

What worksheets are included?

The app includes 24 guided worksheets covering CBT (Thought Record, Mood Journal, Activity Schedule, and more), Sleep (Sleep Diary, Sleep Hygiene, Sleep Restriction, Stimulus Control), ACT (Values Clarification, Values Card Sort), DBT (Emotion Regulation), and Positive Psychology (Gratitude Journal, Best Possible Self, Three Good Things, and more).
